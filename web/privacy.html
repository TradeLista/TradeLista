Privacy

Privacy Policy

What data TradeLista collects, why, and what your rights are.

This page explains our actual data practices in plain language. It is not yet a complete, lawyer-reviewed Datenschutzerklärung/GDPR notice (that also needs our full legal entity details, which will follow separately) — treat it as an accurate starting point, not a final legal document.

What we collect

Only what's needed to run the service:

  • Account info — your email and name, when you create an account.
  • Trading data you enter — trades, notes, tags, reflection answers and screenshots you add to your journal.
  • Billing reference — if you subscribe to Pro, a reference ID linking your account to your Stripe customer record. We never see or store your card details — Stripe handles those directly.
  • Contact messages — your name, email, subject and message, only if you write to us via the Contact page.

Why we collect it

To provide your trading journal (store and show you your own trades and notes), to process your Pro subscription, and to reply when you contact us. We don't use your data for advertising, and we don't sell it.

Who else sees it

A few service providers process data on our behalf, strictly to run TradeLista:

  • Supabase — hosts our database, authentication and file storage.
  • Stripe — processes Pro subscription payments.
  • IONOS — delivers the emails sent through our Contact form.

None of them are permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

Cookies & tracking

We don't use cookies, analytics, or advertising trackers of any kind. The only things stored in your browser are functional (your login session and app preferences) — nothing that tracks you across sites. Fonts are served from our own server rather than a third party's, so no font request ever leaves a trace with anyone else either.

Your rights

You can access, correct, export or delete your trades and notes at any time from within the app. If you'd like your entire account and all associated data erased, or have any other request about your data, reach out via the Contact page — we'll act on it promptly.
