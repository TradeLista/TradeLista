MT5 setup guide

Connect your MT5 account

Attach the TradeLista Expert Advisor once, and every closed trade lands on your calendar automatically. (MT4 support isn't available yet.)

1

Get your account API key

In TradeLista, click your avatar (top right) → Account settings, then scroll to Trading accounts and add a new account. TradeLista generates a unique API key for that account — this is how the EA is matched to your calendar. Keep it private, it's tied to your data only.

2

Download the TradeLista Expert Advisor

Currently available for MT5 only. Download TradeListaSync.mq5. It's a lightweight EA that only reports closed trades — it never places or modifies orders.

3

Install it in MetaTrader

In MetaTrader 5, open File → Open Data Folder, then copy the downloaded file into MQL5 / Experts.

Right-click Navigator → Expert Advisors and choose Refresh so it shows up in the list.

4

Allow WebRequests to the TradeLista API

Go to Tools → Options → Expert Advisors, tick "Allow WebRequest for listed URL", and add:

https://xkmpknoughjnxalkoatx.supabase.co

Without this, MetaTrader silently blocks the EA from sending your closed trades.

5

Attach the EA and paste your API key

Drag TradeListaSync onto any chart (symbol/timeframe doesn't matter — it just listens for closed trades on the account). In the EA's Inputs tab, paste the ApiKey from step 1. Make sure "Allow algorithmic trading" is checked and a smiling face icon appears in the top-right of the chart.

6

Close a trade and check your calendar

The EA fires automatically whenever a trade closes — no manual export, no PDF. Open your Calendar, switch to the matching account, and the trade should appear on the right day within a few seconds.

Troubleshooting
Trades aren't showing up on the calendar
Double-check step 4 — the API URL must be added exactly under "Allow WebRequest for listed URL". Also confirm AutoTrading is enabled (the button in the MetaTrader toolbar should be green, not red).
The EA icon in the top-right shows a sad face
That means AutoTrading is disabled for that chart. Click the AutoTrading button in the toolbar, or right-click the chart → Expert Advisors → "Allow live trading".
Can I connect more than one account?
Free plan: 1 account (manual entry only). Pro plan: up to 5 connected accounts. Repeat steps 1 and 5 for each additional account (each gets its own API key), then switch between them using the account switcher in your avatar menu or the account selector on your Calendar page.
Does the EA place or modify trades?
No — and it's built so that it can't. TradeListaSync contains no order-placement code at all: it only reads your account's closed trade history (symbol, lot size, entry/exit, profit) and reports it to your calendar over a one-way connection. It can't open, close or modify a position, and it never touches your balance, withdrawals, or your MetaTrader login.

The "Allow algorithmic trading" toggle from step 5 can look like you're granting trading permissions — you're not. MetaTrader requires that toggle to be on for any Expert Advisor to run at all, including a purely read-only one like this. It's a MetaTrader-wide switch, not a permission specific to TradeListaSync.

And your API key only authorizes writing that one account's trade data into your own TradeLista calendar — it can't be used to access your broker account, place trades, or touch any other account.
I don't use an EA — can I still use TradeLista?
Yes. Manual entry works on both Free and Pro plans — just add each trade's symbol, lot size, entry, exit and profit/loss straight from the Calendar page.
